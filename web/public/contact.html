Frequently Asked Questions

What is Movie Collector?

Movie Collector is an app designed to help users organize and manage their personal movie collections. You can easily add, categorize, and track the movies you own, want to watch, or have already seen.

How do I add a movie to my collection?

To add a movie, simply search for the title using the search bar, then click "Add to Collection." You can also manually input movie details if it’s not found in our database.

Can I track movies I want to watch?

Yes! You can create a "Watchlist" to track movies you want to watch in the future. Just add them to your watchlist from the movie’s detail page.

Can I export my movie collection?

Yes, you can export your entire movie collection to a CSV file for backup or use in other applications. Go to "Settings" and select the "Export Collection" option.

Is Movie Collector free?

Movie Collector offers a free version with basic features, and a premium version with additional features such as unlimited collection size, advanced sorting, and cloud backup.

How do I contact support?

If you need help, you can contact our support team via the "Help & Support" section in the app, or send an email to support@moviecollectorapp.com.
